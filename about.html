What We Do

We build custom analysis systems centered around urban issues using:
  • state of the art statistical and spatial analysis methods
  • mostly open-source tools
  • open-data
Our work has been featured in The Guardian, The New York Times and Ars Electronica.

The Tools We Use

We specialize in the integration of a wide variety of sophisticated tools used in many different disciplines:
  • Spatial Analysis: PostGIS, QGIS, ArcGIS
  • Statistical Analysis: R
  • Data Visualization: Processing, Javascript
  • Automatic Reporting: LaTeX
  • Languages: Python, Javascript, SQL, PHP
  • Complex Modelling: Vensim, Netlogo
